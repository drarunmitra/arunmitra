## Background

This R-based workshop on Infectious Disease Modeling (IDM) was delivered as the Pre-Conference Workshop on Infectious Disease Modeling at JHAPSMCON-2026, organised by AIIMS Deoghar. It is aimed at learners who want to build practical infectious-disease-modeling skills in R through collaborative, small-group modeling activities.

## Approach

The workshop is organised around group activity scripts (`group1.R` through `group4.R`), with each group working through a guided infectious-disease-modeling exercise in R. The format is collaborative and hands-on, prioritising live coding and peer learning over lecture.

## What we found

- Participants practise reading, running, and adapting R infectious-disease-modeling scripts.
- Group-based structure reinforces collaborative, code-sharing modeling workflows.

## Outputs & impact

- Group activity R scripts (`group1.R`–`group4.R`) used in the sessions.
- Venue: JHAPSMCON-2026, AIIMS Deoghar.
